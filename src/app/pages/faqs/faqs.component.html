FAQ's

How do I register?

Please call into our centre and we will guide you through the registration process.

What happens once I register?

We will directly arrange your free assessment and give you an instant feedback.

How do I know that my child will be safe

All new tutors have to undergo a series of stringent checks, including a full Police check.

What reassurance do I have about the tutors?

Apart from the CRB disclosure, we require all our tutors to provide us with professional and personal references, which we check, and also proof of their qualifications.

How do I know the tutor is up to date with the curriculum?

We require all our tutors to be in a current teaching positions.

Will my child get homework?

Yes, as this is the only way we can check (and consolidate) whether or not they have understood any new concepts taught

How do I cancel a lesson?

You should contact the centre director and let him or her know as soon as you can. For consistency you will have to reschedule the lesson.

What should I do if I don’t want tuition to carry on?

Advice the centre director and we will cancel your contract as required.

Won’t my child be too tired for an hour’s tuition after school?

Private tuition is different from a classroom environment, and can be very stimulating. Of course, it depends on the age of the pupil, but you can arrange tuition times with the centre director so that your child is not too tired to benefit from the lessons.

Will the Centre be able to fit around my child’s commitments/activities?

Our tutors will do all they can to arrange tuition at a suitable time for all concerned. Please talk to the centre director about which times work best for you.

Registration and Terms of Business

Registration

After an initial discussion and assessment with a client we ask them to think carefully about whether they wish us to proceed with the agreed tuition programme. We leave the client to contact the Director when they are ready to register and start tuition.

Holidays and Illness

If it is necessary to cancel a teaching session notice must be given at least 24 hours in advance, in which case no fee will be charged by our tutors. Cancellation with less than 24 hours notice will normally be charged at half the agreed hourly rate. If no notice is given and a session is missed the full fee in lieu of the reserved teaching time will be charged. Please notify the tutor of any holiday dates that will affect the teaching schedule.

Payment for Lessons

Lessons should be paid for at the end of each session or the last week of the month
